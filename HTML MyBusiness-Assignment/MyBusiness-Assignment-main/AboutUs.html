About Us

SCOREBOARD sports was founded in 2010 by a group of sports enthusiasts who recognized a need for high-quality, affordable sports jerseys. The founders had a passion for sports and a desire to provide athletes and fans with a better experience when it came to their sports apparel. They started the business from scratch, designing and producing their first jerseys in a small workshop. Over time, they built a reputation for delivering exceptional quality and service, and the business grew rapidly. Today, SCOREBOARD is one of the leading providers of sports jerseys in the region, serving thousands of customers every year. We are proud of our origins and continue to uphold the same principles of quality, affordability, and customer service that drove our founders to start this business in the first place.



Our Crew

At SCOREBOARD Sports, we believe that our success is driven by our talented and dedicated team of professionals. We currently employ a team of 20 staff members, including designers, production personnel, customer service representatives, and administrative staff. Each member of our team brings a unique set of skills and experience to the table, and we are committed to providing them with a supportive work environment that fosters growth and development. Our team members are passionate about sports and take pride in delivering high-quality products and exceptional customer service. We believe that investing in our staff is the key to our continued success, and we are always looking for ways to improve our training programs and enhance our employees' skills.
